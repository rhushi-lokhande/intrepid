About Intrepid

Intrepid academy is committed to give new hope & development towards students Carrier & Education by creating environment which boost their confidence & enthusiasm to achieve highest possible results. Our teachers are Expert, Dynamic, highly academic & eager to offer the smart quality education.

The moral of INTREPID ACADEMY is to recognize the potential in every student & help them to succeed in their academics & life also.

Our main goal at INTREPID ACADEMY is to create awareness among students towards their carrier. We ensure that our students develop both skills & interest towards their subjects. We aspire to develop successful & confident individuals.

Vision

Intrepid Academy started with a vision to change the complete scenario about how Home Tuition & Classroom Teaching works. We at Intrepid Academy provide best Home Tutor & Classroom Teaching which helps students to understand the subject in a better way & help them to grow in all-round manner.

Mission
